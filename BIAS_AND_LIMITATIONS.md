# Bias and Limitations

Statement on potential biases and limitations of the dataset.

1. **Selection Bias:** Participating institutions were self-selected, which may not represent all educational contexts in Latin America.
2. **Self-Reporting Bias:** Motivation and AI usage data are self-reported and subject to participant subjectivity.
3. **Geographical Coverage:** While multiple countries are included, coverage within those countries may be limited to urban areas.
4. **Temporal Limitation:** The data reflects a specific point in time (2025) and may not capture long-term trends.
5. **Language Barrier:** Surveys were primarily in Spanish and Portuguese, potentially excluding indigenous language speakers.
