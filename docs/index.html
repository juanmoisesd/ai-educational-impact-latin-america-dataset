Key Research Questions

  • What is the correlation between AI usage and academic performance?

    Preliminary analysis shows a moderate positive correlation (r=0.45) suggesting that students who utilize AI tools effectively tend to have higher standardized scores.

  • Are there significant differences in student motivation across LATAM countries?

    Data indicates that students in Brazil and Mexico report slightly higher levels of motivation when using generative AI tools in class compared to the regional average.
