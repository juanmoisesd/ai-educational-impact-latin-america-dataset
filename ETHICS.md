# Ethics Statement

The research conducted to generate this dataset adheres to strict ethical guidelines.

1. **Informed Consent:** All participants provided informed consent before data collection.
2. **Confidentiality:** Measures have been taken to ensure the confidentiality of participant information.
3. **Institutional Review Board (IRB):** The study protocol was reviewed and approved by the relevant ethics committees.
4. **Transparency:** We are committed to transparency in our data collection and analysis methods.
5. **Data Minimization:** Only data necessary for research purposes was collected.
