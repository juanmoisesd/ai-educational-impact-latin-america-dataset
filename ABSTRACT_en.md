# Abstract

This dataset explores the impact of artificial intelligence (AI) on educational processes in Latin America. It focuses on three fundamental pillars: academic performance, student motivation, and self-regulation of learning. Through a rigorous methodology, data has been collected from various countries and educational levels, allowing for a multidimensional analysis of how AI tools are transforming education in the region. The findings provide a solid basis for future research and educational policies aimed at the effective integration of technology in the classroom.
